Let's get the basic things ready

1. Generate a Rent Receipt on online platforms like ClearTax, tax2win, etc, get it signed by the house owner with a 1 Rs revenue stamp attached to it.
2. Transfer the rent amount from your bank account to your owner's bank account.
3. Rental Agreement (Optional but good to have)
Get this only when you don't know the owner personally.
