Why Netflix built an internet speed test website

Yes Please tell me

Here you are asking your Internet Service Provider (ISP - Airtel, Jio, ACT Fibernet, etc) to get the data (Movie 🍿, etc) from the Netflix servers.

What happens when you use Netflix on your phone/laptop

What does ISP will do?

Your ISP will go to the Netflix server, take the content (Movie 🍿, etc), and sends it to your phone/laptop.

Let's build fast.com

Netflix: How can we send the data (Movie 🍿, etc) to the user faster ?

🍿

What Netflix does?

Netflix built a speed test website hosted it on the same place on which the other content (Movie 🍿, etc), was there

Now the ISP wants to show the best speed of their internet on fast.com

ISP now optimize/prioritize fast.com requests to show the best internet speed on the website

📱

Now because the Netflix content (Movie 🍿, etc) and the fast.com are at the same place you get faster access to Netflix content.
